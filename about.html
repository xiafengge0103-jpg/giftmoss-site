A warm craft brand built for global B2B buyers.

GiftMoss is a handmade gifts and home decor manufacturer based in Ningbo, China. We specialize in OEM custom resin figurines, wooden decor, candle holders, woven baskets and gift packaging — designed for importers, wholesalers, retail chains and e-commerce brands worldwide.

Our story

From handmade craftsmanship to scalable retail supply.

We started with a simple belief: handmade gifts should be accessible to retailers worldwide without compromising on quality or practicality. Our team combines traditional craft techniques with modern production management to deliver collections that are warm, giftable and retail-ready.

Every product category — from resin figurines to woven baskets — is developed with B2B buyers in mind: consistent quality, clear MOQ expectations, and full OEM customization support.

What we offer global buyers.

Six capabilities that make GiftMoss a reliable manufacturing partner for importers, retailers and brands.

Original Design

Warm, giftable styles adaptable across seasonal markets — Christmas, Easter, housewarming and everyday decor.

Full OEM / ODM

Custom mold, material, size, color, logo, hangtag, barcode and packaging — we build your private label from scratch.

Mixed Category Sourcing

Combine resin, wood, ceramic, candle accessories and packaging in one seasonal order — one supplier, one shipment.

Sample Development

Reference idea → design suggestion → sample photos → confirmation → bulk production. Clear process, no surprises.

Quality Control

In-line QC during production and pre-shipment inspection ensure consistent finish across your entire order.

Export Ready

SKU-level packing, sea/air/express shipping options, and clear documentation for customs clearance.
